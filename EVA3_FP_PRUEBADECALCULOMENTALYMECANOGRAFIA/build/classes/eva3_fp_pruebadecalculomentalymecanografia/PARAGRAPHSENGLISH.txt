As he moved between the tall bookcases, he discovered a novel with a handwritten note inside. The message, though short, felt deeply personal and made him curious about the person who wrote it.

She wrapped herself in a blanket and sank into the couch, listening to the soft hum of the storm. Sometimes, she thought, rainy days were the perfect excuse to pause and breathe.
